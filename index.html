A brief introduction about myself


Hello, I'm Amulek Angulo, a passionate and dedicated Software Developer with a strong drive to create efficient and impactful software solutions. I'm actively seeking an internship to gain practical experience and further enhance my skills in the field.


Over the past 4 years, I have acquired extensive knowledge of programming languages such as Python, PHP, JavaScript, and C#, as well as databases like MySQL, PL/SQL, and MS SQL Server. Additionally, I have experience working with frameworks such as Angular and .NET, using them to create, maintain, and write software for various personal projects. Alongside technical expertise, I have also developed valuable interpersonal skills including leadership, communication, emotional intelligence, teamwork, and business management.


I find great satisfaction in fixing bugs and writing clean software, as it enables better collaboration among team members and ensures a positive experience for end users. I am also passionate about implementing efficient algorithms to enhance overall performance as projects scale over time. Continual learning is a personal objective of mine, as I believe there is always room for improvement. I strive to grow as a Software Developer and as an individual, both by assisting others and seeking guidance from my peers. I am eager to work for a company that fosters knowledge expansion and personal and professional development and allows me to apply my skills to achieve exceptional outcomes.

If you would like to learn more about me, let's connect on LinkedIn to continue the conversation.
